# Content Strategy & Copywriting

## Brand Voice

### Tone Attributes
- **Professional but approachable** - Expert without being intimidating
- **Tech-forward** - Embrace cyberpunk aesthetic in language
- **Confident** - Show capability without arrogance
- **Action-oriented** - Focus on results and outcomes
- **Concise** - Respect visitor's time

### Language Style
- Use active voice
- Short sentences and paragraphs
- Technical terms when appropriate (audience is tech-savvy)
- Occasional cyberpunk terminology ("initialize", "execute", "system", "protocol")
- Avoid buzzwords and fluff
- Numbers and specifics over vague claims

## A/B Testing Ideas

### Hero Headlines
- Test technical vs. benefit-focused
- Test with/without cyberpunk styling
- Test length (short vs. descriptive)

### CTA Buttons
- "Talk to my AI" vs. "Get Started"
- "Hire Me" vs. "Start a Project"
- Button color: cyan vs. magenta

### Chatbot Greeting
- Formal vs. casual tone
- With/without quick replies
- Immediate lead capture vs. conversation first

### Project Descriptions
- Technical details vs. business outcomes
- Short vs. detailed
- With/without metrics

## Voice & Tone Guidelines

### DO:
✅ Use active voice
✅ Be specific with numbers and timelines
✅ Show, don't tell (demos over descriptions)
✅ Use technical terms appropriately
✅ Be confident but humble
✅ Focus on outcomes and results

### DON'T:
❌ Use buzzwords ("synergy", "disruptive", "revolutionary")
❌ Make vague claims ("best", "fastest", "most innovative")
❌ Oversell or exaggerate
❌ Use jargon without context
❌ Write long paragraphs
❌ Be overly formal or robotic
